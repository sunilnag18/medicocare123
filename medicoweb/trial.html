MINISTRY OF AYUSH - MEDICO CARE

About the Ministry of AYUSH

The Ministry of AYUSH was formed to ensure the development and propagation of Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy systems of healthcare. It strives to promote holistic healthcare across India, integrating the traditional practices with modern medicine for a healthier society.

Upcoming Schemes

The Ministry of AYUSH is launching a number of new healthcare initiatives aimed at improving access to alternative and holistic medicine. These schemes focus on rural outreach, promoting indigenous healthcare knowledge, and integrating traditional methods with modern healthcare practices. The government is investing significantly in research, education, and infrastructure to make AYUSH-based healthcare available to everyone.

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi

Under the leadership of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, the government has made significant strides in promoting AYUSH healthcare systems. His vision of integrating traditional medicine with modern healthcare practices has enabled millions of people to access affordable healthcare. The promotion of Yoga on an international scale is one of his most notable contributions to global wellness.
